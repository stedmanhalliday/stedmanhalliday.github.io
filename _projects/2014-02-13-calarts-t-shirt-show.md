The CalArts Graphic Design program's annual T-Shirt Show turns the department into a print shop for a night. For the 2014 edition I designed and built the promotional website—a catalog of roughly 75 shirt designs across three colorways, each crediting its designer—and produced the printed labels for the show itself.

The site lets visitors switch between white, cream, and yellow colorways to browse the full lineup of student and faculty designs (Ian Lynam, Mr. Keedy, Jenny Earnest, and many others). I built it as a PHP and LESS site set in Apercu, and produced a 74-page print PDF of T-shirt labels for the event.
