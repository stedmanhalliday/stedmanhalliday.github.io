A set of motion-graphics and meme-format clips for an Old Spice "No Sweat" campaign produced with Super Deluxe. The work leaned into late-2010s internet vernacular—a blue-screen-of-death bit, a "spicy wave" title treatment, clip-art poll animations—matching the brand's deadpan-absurd voice to the formats people were actually sharing.

I animated and assembled the spots for social distribution.
