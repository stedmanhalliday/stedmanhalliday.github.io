Design Index is a digital publication: a reference on graphic design itself—movements, designers, schools, and the principles of type and composition. I designed it as a tablet-native book, with profile spreads (Saul Bass), anatomy-of-type pages, and a consistent editorial system tying the reference together.

It treats design history as something you'd want to browse, not just look up.
