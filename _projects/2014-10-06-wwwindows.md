[wwwindows](http://stedmanrh.github.io/wwwindows) is a web experience that deconstructs the window—the interface element—as an idea. Five sections trace its precursors, origins, and evolution, each presented inside a window styled after the operating system of its era, so the chrome itself walks forward in time as you read.

The windows are also the navigation: deliberately obtrusive, overlapping, and in your way, which is the point. It's a small argument about a metaphor we stopped noticing, built in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
