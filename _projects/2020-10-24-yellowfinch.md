Yellowfinch is a startup using IoT sensors to model roof-collapse and structural risk. I named the company and designed its identity and pitch deck as it went through an accelerator—work that ended up on a Nasdaq tower in Times Square.

The mark and logotype had to read as both technical and approachable: infrastructure you'd actually trust on your building.
