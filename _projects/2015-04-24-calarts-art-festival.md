The CalArts Art Festival is a two-day celebration of the school's art-making across disciplines. I designed the identity and printed collateral for the 2015 edition: a flexible system meant to hold work as varied as the community it represents, applied across fliers, cards, and a printed lineup.

The festival is built around interdisciplinary and collaborative work, so the identity stays loose enough to frame anything without flattening it. Watch the [festival recap](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-BD0nMLSjI).
