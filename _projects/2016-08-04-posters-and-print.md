A grouping of standalone print pieces. The Black in AmeriKKKa tour fliers (Osiris & the Ubiquitous Love Tribe, summer 2016) layer African-textile texture over concentric type; the Greg Victoroff identity is a serif monogram and card system for an attorney; the Minnesota "Land of 10,000 Lakes" atlas is an illustrated infographic spread; and Top Rmn is a black-and-white photo zine of party candids.

Different clients and formats, collected here as one body of print work.
