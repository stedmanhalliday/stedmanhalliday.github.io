For CalArts Halloween 2015 I led design on the poster campaign for the school's annual haunt, themed as a carnival freakshow. The "Strongman" hero poster (with Katie Barger) anchored a series of nine typographic posters I designed and wrote to complement the scenic prosthetics built by the CalArts Themed Entertainment Association.

Each poster is a different act, played straight across a consistent typographic system.
