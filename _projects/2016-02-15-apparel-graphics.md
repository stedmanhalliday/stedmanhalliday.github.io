A collection of apparel and merchandise graphics from the mid-2010s. Clef Joint sets a halftone treble clef on a black disc for a music project; the $HEEZU$ tote flips a Yeezus-style Venus symbol into a feminist riff; the Lovelace tee stacks a heavy-metal logotype over halftone bird photography. Small, self-contained pieces, each a graphic built to live on a body.
